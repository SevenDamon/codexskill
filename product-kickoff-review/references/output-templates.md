# Output Templates

Use these as concise structures. Adapt to the project; do not fill with generic fluff.

## Commercial Verdict

```markdown
**Commercial Verdict**
Verdict: GO / NARROW / VALIDATE / STOP

Paying customer:
End user:
Reason to pay:
Current workaround:
Save-money / make-money logic:
Price hypothesis:
Repeat-purchase reason:
Sharpest failure risk:
The single most important thing to validate next:
```
